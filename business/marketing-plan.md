# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Skill-scribe: AI-powered coding skills analysis and improvement tool for developers.
Alternatives:
1. CodeCraft: Personalized coding coach for professional growth.
2. DevBoost: Intelligent coding skills assessment and enhancement platform.
3. CodeMentor: AI-driven coding skills analysis and mentorship tool.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Alex Chen**: Full-stack developer at a mid-sized startup, daily job involves coding and reviewing code, budget $100-$300/month for productivity tools.
2. **Maya Patel**: Lead developer at a large enterprise, daily job involves managing a team of developers and ensuring high-quality code, budget $500-$1000/month for team productivity tools.
3. **Ethan Kim**: Freelance developer, daily job involves working on various projects and constantly improving coding skills, budget $50-$200/month for personal productivity tools.

## Channels
1. **Social Media (LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub)**: CAC estimate $50-$100, targeting developer communities and groups.
2. **Influencer Marketing (Developer Influencers)**: CAC estimate $100-$300, partnering with influencers to promote skill-scribe.
3. **Content Marketing (Blog, YouTube, Podcast)**: CAC estimate $20-$50, creating high-quality content to attract developers.

## Content Cadence
Week 1-2: Introduction to skill-scribe and its benefits
Week 3-4: Tutorial series on using skill-scribe for coding skills improvement
Week 5-6: Success stories and testimonials from early adopters
Week 7-8: Advanced features and tips for getting the most out of skill-scribe
Week 9-10: Industry trends and how skill-scribe can help developers stay ahead
Week 11-12: Special promotions and discounts for loyal customers

## Launch Milestones
* D-30: Finalize marketing plan and content calendar
* D-0: Launch skill-scribe and start promoting on social media and influencer channels
* D+30: Analyze early adoption metrics and adjust marketing strategy as needed
* D+90: Review success metrics and plan for future growth and expansion

## Success Metrics
* DAU (Daily Active Users): 1,000 by D+30, 5,000 by D+90
* $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) target: $10,000 by D+30, $50,000 by D+90
