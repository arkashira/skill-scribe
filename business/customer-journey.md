# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for skill-scribe outlines the phases a potential customer goes through from initial awareness to expansion of the product. The map highlights trigger events, friction points, user emotions, opportunities to delight, and key metrics for each phase.

## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: Social media ads, online forums, or word-of-mouth recommendations about AI-powered coding skills analysis tools.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about the benefits of AI-driven coding skills improvement, skepticism about the effectiveness of such tools.
* **User Emotions**: Curiosity, interest in self-improvement.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials, demos, or introductory webinars showcasing the tool's capabilities.
* **Metric**: Number of unique website visitors, social media engagement (e.g., likes, shares, comments).

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Positive reviews, testimonials, or case studies from existing users, highlighting the tool's impact on their coding skills.
* **Friction Points**: Comparison with competing products, concerns about pricing, integration with existing workflows.
* **User Emotions**: Evaluation, consideration of alternatives, weighing pros and cons.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized product demos, free trials with limited features, responsive customer support.
* **Metric**: Number of free trial sign-ups, product comparison page views.

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: Signing up for a free trial or a limited-time offer, allowing users to experience the tool firsthand.
* **Friction Points**: Onboarding process, initial complexity of the tool, limited features in the free trial.
* **User Emotions**: Excitement to try something new, potential frustration with the learning curve.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Interactive tutorials, in-app guidance, timely and helpful support.
* **Metric**: Free trial conversion rate, average time spent using the tool during the trial period.

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Purchase of a subscription or a one-time license, indicating a commitment to using the tool.
* **Friction Points**: Payment processing issues, initial setup and configuration challenges.
* **User Emotions**: Satisfaction with the decision, anticipation of improved coding skills.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Welcome packages, personalized onboarding, regular updates with new features.
* **Metric**: Customer acquisition cost (CAC), customer retention rate.

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Regular use of the tool, achieving significant improvements in coding skills, or exploring additional features.
* **Friction Points**: Limited scalability, lack of advanced features for power users.
* **User Emotions**: Loyalty, appreciation for the tool's impact on their career or projects.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Premium support, exclusive access to new features, community recognition.
* **Metric**: Customer lifetime value (CLV), net promoter score (NPS).

By understanding and addressing the needs, emotions, and pain points at each phase of the customer journey, skill-scribe can tailor its marketing, sales, and support strategies to provide a seamless and delightful experience, ultimately driving user adoption and loyalty.
